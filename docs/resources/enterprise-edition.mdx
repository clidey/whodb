# Enterprise Edition

WhoDB Enterprise Edition is the commercial package for teams that need more than the open-source Community Edition.

## What It Adds

Enterprise Edition is aimed at organizations that need a broader supported surface around WhoDB. Depending on the package, that can include:

- Additional source connectors beyond the CE catalog.
- Broader coverage for enterprise databases, warehouses, cloud services, or JDBC-backed sources.
- Commercial support and packaging for teams that need a vendor-backed path.
- Deployment options that fit internal platform, desktop, CLI, or automation workflows.
- The same general WhoDB workflow model: connect, inspect, query, visualize, import/export, and automate where supported.

## When To Evaluate It

Consider Enterprise Edition when:

- CE does not cover a database or service your team needs.
- You need a commercial support path for production usage.
- Your organization wants a standardized internal database workspace instead of many unrelated tools.
- You want WhoDB-style workflows across a larger or more specialized data stack.
